## IP Blocking

Let's open up [archive.org](https://archive.org):

![archive_safari](/img/internet-censorship-in-kazakhstan-part-1/archive_safari.png 'Archive.org in Safari')

Okay, Safari can't even tell what's wrong with the connection so it just times out.
Let's see what's happening under the hood via `traceroute`:

```bash
$ traceroute archive.org

traceroute to archive.org (207.241.224.2), 64 hops max, 52 byte packets
 1  192.168.1.1 (192.168.1.1)  2.005 ms  1.308 ms  1.215 ms
 2  82.200.242.199 (82.200.242.199)  1.646 ms  1.606 ms  3.444 ms
 3  82.200.243.116 (82.200.243.116)  56.544 ms  54.231 ms  59.570 ms
 4  * * *
 5  * * *
 6  * * *
 7  * * *
```

You can tell that it gets stuck at `82.200.243.116` which belongs to "JSC Kazakhtelecom".
